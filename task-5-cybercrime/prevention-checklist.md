# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## ✅ General Safety Measures
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or banking details with anyone  
- Always verify the sender before clicking on links in emails or messages  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for important accounts  
- Check website URLs carefully before entering sensitive information  
- Do not download files or apps from untrusted sources  
- Always verify UPI payment requests before approving them  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes, especially for receiving money  
- Be cautious of calls claiming to be from banks or officials  
- Immediately report cyber fraud on https://cybercrime.gov.in or call the cybercrime helpline number 1930 in case of financial fraud  
- Regularly monitor your bank transactions and alerts
