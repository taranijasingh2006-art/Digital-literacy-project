# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Use strong and secure passwords
- Keep your profiles professional
- Verify information before sharing
- Respect others online
- Adjust privacy settings regularly
- Use official and secure website for transactions

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal information (OTP, passwords)
- Do not post offensive or harmful content
- Do not accept requests from unknown people
- Do not click on suspicious links
- Do not overshare private details
- Do not reuse the same password across multiple accounts
